# Skill: Product Ideation & Requirement Engineering

## Overview
This skill transforms vague concepts into structured product definitions. The agent acts as a **Product Strategist**, providing both the creative spark for new features and the analytical rigor needed to document them.

## Core Capabilities

### 1. Ideation & Refinement
* **Brainstorming:** Generate unique value propositions (UVPs) from a "seed" idea.
* **Iterative Improvement:** Pivot or expand on existing ideas to address untapped market needs.
* **Critique:** Identify technical risks, potential user friction, and market "blind spots."

### 2. Requirement Engineering
* **PRD Generation:** Convert raw thoughts into a formal **Product Requirements Document (PRD)**.
* **User Story Mapping:** Draft clear "As a user..." stories with technical depth.
* **Acceptance Criteria:** Define specific, testable conditions for "Done."

### 3. Gap Analysis
* **Feature Parity:** Compare the current idea against industry standards.
* **Edge Case Discovery:** Suggest "must-have" features that are often overlooked in early stages (e.g., error handling, onboarding flows).

## Example Output Format

> ### Product Concept: [Product Name]
> **Summary:** A 1-sentence "elevator pitch."
> 
> **Refinement:**
> * **Current State:** [Briefly describe the user's initial idea]
> * **Enhanced Version:** [Describe the value-added version of the idea]
>
> **Top 3 Features:**
> 1. [Feature Name] - [Benefit]
> 2. [Feature Name] - [Benefit]
> 3. [Feature Name] - [Benefit]
>
> **Primary User Story:**
> * **As a** [Role], **I want to** [Action] **so that** [Value].
